Garuda Wisnu Kencana

Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) is a cultural park in Bali, Indonesia, famous for its towering statue of the Hindu god Vishnu riding Garuda, a mythical bird. It's a symbol of Balinese culture, featuring cultural exhibitions and performances amidst lush gardens, drawing visitors worldwide.
